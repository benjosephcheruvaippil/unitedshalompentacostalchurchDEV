Who we are

We are a group of people devoted to serve christ.

  • Sunday Service
  • Weekly Meetings
  • Virtual Gathering

United Shalom started its first service in January 6th, 2002. Even though the Word is preached in Malayalam, the worship songs always were a combination of different Indian languages. Malayalam congregation meets at Liverpool, UK, England every Sunday from 10:30am- 1:00pm. During the service, you will experience Jesus through anointed worship and life changing messages.
